Who We Are

Enxy Beauty &
Barbering Academy

Founded in April 2026 from the mother company called Enxy Unisex Salon and Spa which was founded in the year 2018, Enxy Beauty and Barbering Academy is a cosmetology training institution Headquartered on the Nungua Highway, Greater Accra. At Enxy Beauty and Barbering Academy, we train (Hair Technology, Beauty Therapy,Nail Technology, Massage and Body Therapy, Hair Dressing, Beauty Specialty and Barbering) Enxy Academy offer full certification programs starting from Normal certificate courses to advance certificates courses. As an widely recognize institution, we pride ourselves of non-discrimination in any form and admit students without regard to race, religion, gender, age, educational background, nationality and/or the physically challenged. The Academy offers tuition in 3 major forms of educational categories and 7 academic departments. They include; (formal, non- formal and informal). These are designed to meet student needs to provide exactly what they expect and their career objectives to fit into the modern industries. As part of our training, we organize internships and collaborate with institutions, churches, organization, schools to have outreach programs for our students. This is done to give our students a feel of the job market before graduation.. These also helps to boost their confidence and gives them exposure to the market industry. FORMAL This category consist of individuals who have completed Junior High School (JHS) and such students start from Semester One (1)  with full generics base on the selected course leading to Tertiary education or start their own businesses or be the best choice for companies. NON-FORMAL This is designed for people with no formal Education background. The Local language is blended with English language for clearer understanding of the course. This class enjoys 10% of theory and 90% demonstration taught in class and practice what they have learnt during internship in the salon INFORMAL Students are trained more on practical, thus they spend most of their time in the salon and production units. SKILL ENHANCEMENT These courses are designed for already practitioners and those who aspire improve their skill in beauty, hair and other areas they fall short. These courses can be offered by, corporate workers, tertiary students, SHS graduates, partially literate and even illiterates who which to improve their skills. After their training, they are allowed 3 weeks’ internship in the salon to perfect what they have learnt.                     COSMETOLOGY This Academy runs the following programs • Hair Technology (Training in hair only) • Beauty Therapy (Training in skin and nails) • Barbering (Training in male and Female grooming) Hair Dressing ( Training in everything hair ) Massage and Body Therapy ( Training in Spa )
